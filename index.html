CCMIC Chess Center, located in Gampaha, Sri Lanka, is a leading hub for nurturing chess talent and promoting excellence in the game. Known for producing world-class and top-ranked players, our center offers a professional learning environment supported by experienced coaches and structured training programs. We are committed to guiding students from beginner level to competitive mastery, helping them build strategic thinking, discipline, and confidence. Through tournaments, workshops, and personalized coaching, CMIC continues to shape the next generation of champions.
